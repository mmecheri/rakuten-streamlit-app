In this section, we present an interface that allows you to make predictions.
Predictions can be made using a model based on Text data, Image data, or both combined.  
</br>

- **Text-based classification**: Conv1D and Simple DNN  
- **Image-based classification**: Xception and InceptionV3  
- **Bimodal classification - Text and Images**:</br>  
	- Conv1D, Simple DNN and Xception  
	- Conv1D, Simple DNN and InceptionV3
